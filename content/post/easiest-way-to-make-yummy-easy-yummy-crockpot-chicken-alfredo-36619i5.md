### Stages Cooking Easy Yummy Crock-Pot Chicken Alfredo

1. Boil and pull apart chicken.



1. Prepare pasta.



1. In a microwave safe bowl combine Cream of Mushroom and chicken, and milk with  Alfredo sauce..
