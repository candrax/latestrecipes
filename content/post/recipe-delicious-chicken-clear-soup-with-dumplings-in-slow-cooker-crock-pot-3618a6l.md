### Ingredients for Chicken Clear Soup with Dumplings in Slow Cooker (Crock pot)

1. Needed 250 gr : chicken filet, cut into pieces.

### Process Cooking Chicken Clear Soup with Dumplings in Slow Cooker (Crock pot)
